Open For Whom

We find ourselves at a critical moment.

We are transitioning to a system for sharing knowledge that is open by default. The decisions we make now will fundamentally shape the future of research and education for many years to come. Asking “open for whom?”, and acting on the answers will determine if we’ll let it replicate old inequities in new ways, or realize the vision of open.
Here we ask the questions.
